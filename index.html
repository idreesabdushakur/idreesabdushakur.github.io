About The Artist

Abdushakur is an artist based in New York City and The UK whose practice is rooted in iconoclasm. He strives to shed light on the traditional aspects of abstract art from a spiritual perspective and its intrinsic importance. He creates meaningful pieces that speak to the core concepts of “sabr” (patience) and “tawheed” (monotheism). Through his creative work, Abdushakur attempts to exalt Allah while exploring the power of abstraction’s aesthetic and expressive potential. Abdushakur creates abstract works that embrace the beauty and sanctity of Islamic Art while maintaining and adhering to a modern, contemporary aesthetic. He invites viewers to reflect on their own spiritual understanding and connection to The Creator within the nuances of his artwork.
