About The Artist

Abdushakur is a Latin-American artist from NYC whose work is characterized by it’s strict adherence to an iconoclast approach. His aim is to illuminate the traditional aesthetics of abstract art and its significance from a spiritual and intrinsic perspective. He produces profound works that express the fundamental concepts of sabr (patience) and al-laanihayiyyah (infiniteness). Through his work, Abdushakur attempts to glorify al-Khalaq (The Creator) while exploring the expressive and aesthetic potential of abstraction. Abdushakur produces abstract art that embraces the beauty and preserves the sanctity of Islamic Art while maintaining a modern, contemporary aesthetic. With the nuanced and meticulous method implemented for his artwork, he compels the khalq (creation) to consider their own spiritual awareness and relationship to al-Khalaq (The Creator).

As an experienced graphic designer and brand developer, Abdushakur is passionate about helping your business realize the full potential of its brand identity and/or rebranding initiatives. Abdushakur specializes in logo design, brand development and graphic design for commercial graphics, creating impactful visual identities that you can believe in. From concept to completion, he puts in the unique and extraordinary effort required to create eye-catching, attention-grabbing graphics that are on point and on-brand. Abdushakur uses his diverse skillset and personal experiences to help your company differentiate, attract, and retain customers with alluring visuals that not only look great, but also communicate your brand's story. His goal is to create effective graphic designs that bring out your brand's true essence.
